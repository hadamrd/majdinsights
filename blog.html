What is the best morning routine to start the day off right?

I get up every morning and it’s going to be a great day. You never know when it’s going to be over, so I refuse to have a bad day. – Paul Henderson

We see more and more testimonials inspired by the cult book The Miracle Morning by Hal Elrod ( read the summary ), which tell how to change your life by getting up earlier, or even very early. If we are often tempted by this morning way of life, many are cooled by the idea of ​​the alarm clock which rings at 5.30 am in the morning… However it is not necessary to follow the precepts of the guides to the letter, the important c is to get inspired and create your own morning routine. Whether you get up an hour earlier than your usual alarm clock, whether you go for a run or to take care of yourself, the important thing is to create a special moment.

Example of a good morning routine:

  1. Get up
  2. Meditate
  3. Take breakfast
  4. Workout
  5. Take a shower
  6. Brush your teats
  7. Get to work
